Lymphangitis

"Lymphangitis is an inflammation of the lymph vessels"

SYMPTOMS:

  • chills
  • swollen lymph glands
  • fever
  • malaise, or a general ill feeling
  • loss of appetite
  • headache
  • aching muscles

CAUSES:

  • diabetes
  • immunodeficiency, or loss of immune function
  • chronic steroid use
  • chickenpox
